## What SIT-HR does

SIT-HR is an system-generated HR advisory tool designed to help managers and business owners navigate employment law, HR process, and people management in England and Wales. It is a guidance tool, not a legal advice service.

## What data we collect and why

### Account data
When you register for SIT-HR, we collect your email address and a password (stored as a secure hash). This data is necessary to provide you with access to the platform. The legal basis for processing this data is the performance of a contract with you.

### Conversation data
The queries you submit and the responses generated are stored in our database, linked to your account. This allows you to access your conversation history and generate export packs. The legal basis for processing this data is the performance of a contract with you.

We store this data for as long as your account is active. If you delete a conversation, it is removed from our systems within 30 days.

### Case reference data
Conversations are linked to case references. Our terms of use require that you do not enter personally identifiable information - including employee names, National Insurance numbers, dates of birth, or addresses - into the platform. Case references should be used in place of any personal identifiers.

If you do enter personal data about a third party into the platform contrary to these terms, LWBC Solutions Ltd is not responsible for that data entering the AI processing pipeline.

### Technical data
We collect standard technical data including IP addresses, browser type, and access timestamps for security and platform stability purposes. The legal basis is our legitimate interests in maintaining a secure and functional service.

## How your data is processed

### Supabase
Your account data and conversation history are stored on Supabase, a database and authentication platform. Supabase acts as a data processor on our behalf. Data is stored in the EU West region. Supabase's privacy policy is available at supabase.com/privacy.

### Anthropic
Queries submitted through SIT-HR are sent to Anthropic's API to generate AI responses. Anthropic acts as a data processor on our behalf. We have a data processing agreement in place with Anthropic. Anthropic does not use API inputs to train its models by default. Anthropic's privacy policy is available at anthropic.com/privacy.

You must not submit special category data - including health data, disability information, or any other sensitive personal data about employees - through this platform. The platform is designed for anonymised case management using case references only.
